Draft a research grant proposal for: $ARGUMENTS

Use the following skills (read each SKILL.md before using):

1. **Research context** — Use the literature-review skill approach to establish the state of the art. Use the pubmed-database and openalex-database skills for key references.

2. **Hypothesis & aims** — Use the hypothesis-generation skill to refine research hypotheses. Use the scientific-brainstorming skill to develop specific aims and innovative approaches.

3. **Grant structure** — Use the research-grants skill for guidance on grant structure, formatting, and funder-specific requirements.

4. **Methodology** — Draft a detailed research plan with feasibility considerations. Reference relevant skills for proposed methods (e.g., scanpy for single-cell work, rdkit for cheminformatics).

5. **Critical evaluation** — Use the scientific-critical-thinking skill to identify potential weaknesses, alternative approaches, and risk mitigation strategies.

6. **Budget justification** — Outline personnel, equipment, supplies, and other costs with justification.

7. **Scholar evaluation** — Use the scholar-evaluation skill to assess the track record of proposed collaborators or to position the PI's expertise.

8. **Document** — Use the docx document skill to create a formatted grant proposal. Include: specific aims page, research strategy (significance, innovation, approach), timeline, budget, and references.

Save the grant proposal to the working directory.
